# JobBridge Homepage Video Montage Brief

## Deliverable
- **Length:** 30 seconds total
- **Structure:** 6 clips × 5 seconds each
- **Output:** `MP4`, `H.264`, `3840x2160`, `30fps`
- **Visual style:** Live-action only, cinematic, ultra-realistic, natural textures, film grain, practical lighting
- **Strictly prohibited:** CGI, 3D animation, surreal AI artifacts, plastic skin, impossible camera motion, fake UI overlays
- **Brand intent:** A cohesive "one-woman show" visual thesis showing creativity, craft, production, operations, and sales in one integrated business

---

## Master Creative Direction
Create a premium, ultra-realistic live-action homepage montage that communicates a founder-led creative business spanning fashion design, graphic design, catering/baking, photography, operations, and digital sales. Every frame must feel tactile and grounded in the real world: natural daylight, believable indoor practicals, visible material textures, subtle lens breathing, authentic human hand movement, and restrained film grain.

The edit should feel like one continuous brand story told through hard cuts and match-cuts. The subject should read as one capable business owner moving across different disciplines with confidence and precision.

### Audio Direction
Layer authentic ambient sound under a subtle royalty-free acoustic guitar/piano bed.

Suggested ambient sound moments:
- pencil on tracing paper
- sewing machine hum and needle strikes
- stylus tapping tablet glass
- flour sweep and countertop contact
- cake knife slice through sponge and icing
- camera shutter and studio light adjustment clicks
- page flip/planner tap
- inventory rustle on shelves
- handshake and fabric movement
- smartphone tap/scroll sound

No dramatic cinematic booms. Keep it premium, human, modern, and restrained.

### Editing Direction
- Use **hard cuts** or **match cuts** only
- No fade-to-black
- No flashy transitions
- Pace should feel efficient and intentional
- Let each 5-second segment have one dominant visual action
